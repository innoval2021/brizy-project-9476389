About us

Find out more about the history of Edlons, Thokomola Air Conditioning & Refrigeration, what we stand for and where we are heading in our business as well as our business principle in how we operate.

A Brief History

​TP Air conditioners and Refrigeration, previously known as Edlons Air-conditioning and Refrigeration has been operating as a company since 2007 involved in the air conditioning installation and service as well as industrial and commercial refrigeration. The success of our business is built on the strong foundation of service excellence and our installation, service, and maintenance. No matter how big or small your air-conditioning requirements, the engineering expertise and commitment of TP Air conditioners and Refrigeration will satisfy your requirement.


Our Goals

​As TP Air conditioners and refrigeration is not limited to any restrictive trading agreements, we are in the position to supply the equipment that best suit your application, be it from: Samsung, Carrier, York, Daikin, TCL, etc. The current owner Mrs. U Naidoo has owned the company since 2011. TP Air conditioners and Refrigeration is a proudly a B-BBEE-Level: 1 company, 100% black owned by women, through the department of trade and industry.


Our Business Principle

  • We are committed to diversity in a working environment where there is a mutual trust and where everyone feels responsible for the performance of our company.
  • We conduct our operations with honesty, integrity and openness and with respect for the human rights and interest of our employees.
  • TP Air-conditioners and Refrigeration believes in being legislatively compliant with special emphasis on safety and the environment.
  • We are registered with Workmen’s compensation, Unemployment’s Insurance Fund, Construction Industry Development Fund (cidb), The South African Institute of Refrigeration and Air-conditioning (SAIRAC).
